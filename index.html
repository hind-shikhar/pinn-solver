How It Works

Three steps to your solution

01

Write Your Equation

Type your ODE or PDE using intuitive notation. Use u_x, u_xx, u_t for derivatives. Define domain bounds, boundary conditions, and parameters.

02

Neural Network Trains

A deep neural network is trained to satisfy your equation at thousands of randomly sampled collocation points. It minimises both the PDE residual and boundary condition errors simultaneously.

03

Visualise the Solution

Watch the solution evolve live. For ODEs you get a smooth curve; for PDEs you get an interactive heatmap or 3D surface — updated every few epochs as the network converges.

Features

Everything you need to explore PINNs

✏️

User-Defined Equations

Type any ODE or PDE using symbolic notation. The app auto-detects equation type and available variables.

⚡

Live In-Browser Training

All computation runs on your GPU via WebGL — no server, no install. Training updates the plot every few epochs.

📊

Interactive Visualisation

ODE solutions as smooth curves; PDE solutions as animated heatmaps or 3D surfaces. Zoomable and downloadable.

🎯

Flexible Boundary Conditions

Add as many Dirichlet or Neumann BCs as you need. Expressions support sin, cos, exp, and custom parameters.

🔬

Automatic Differentiation

Derivatives are computed exactly via TensorFlow.js GradientTape — up to second order (∂²u/∂x² etc.).
